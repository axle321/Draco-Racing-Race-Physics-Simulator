Physics Model Reference
CO₂ Gas Expulsion Model
Isentropic choked flow (de Laval nozzle):
ṁ = A_t · P · √(γ/RT) · (2/(γ+1))^((γ+1)/(2(γ-1)))

Thrust: F = ṁ · v_e · η
v_e = √(2γ/(γ-1) · RT₀/M · [1-(Pe/P₀)^((γ-1)/γ)])

Pressure decay (adiabatic):
P(t) = P₀ · (1 - ṁ·t / m₀)^γ

CO₂ constants: γ=1.30, M=44 g/mol, R=188.9 J/(kg·K)
Rolling Resistance
Contact patch deformation:
F_rr = C_rr · N
C_rr ≈ (a/R) where a = contact half-width

Bearing friction torque:
τ_bearing = μ_k · F_N · r_axle
F_bearing = τ_bearing / r_wheel

Rolling radius:
r_eff = r_wheel - δ (elastic deflection)
δ ≈ F_N / (E* · l_contact) (Hertzian contact)
Rotational Inertia
Effective mass including wheel inertia:
m_eff = m_car + 4 · (k · m_wheel · (r_wheel/r_wheel)²)
m_eff = m_car + 4 · k · m_wheel

For Draco 3-spoke hub-heavy wheel:
k ≈ 0.52 → m_eff = m_car + 4 × 0.52 × m_wheel

Crossover speed (v_c):
When F_accel = F_inertia_rot → rim mass disadvantage
Aerodynamic Drag
Drag force:
F_D = ½ · ρ · v² · C_d · A

ρ_air = 1.204 kg/m³ @ 20°C, 1 atm
Scales as: F_D ∝ v²

At v = 20 m/s (Draco target):
F_D = ½ × 1.204 × 400 × C_d × A
= 240.8 × C_d × A

Downforce effect on rolling resistance:
N_total = mg + F_downforce
F_rr_aero = C_rr · F_downforce
Tether Line Friction
Guide friction force:
F_tether = μ_guide · T · (1 - cos θ)

where T = tether tension, θ = deflection angle

Lateral constraint force:
At angle α from centreline:
F_lateral = T · sin α
F_friction_guide = μ · F_lateral

Two guides (front + rear):
F_tether_total = 2 × F_tether_single
Equation of Motion (ODE)
Numerical integration (RK4):
m_eff · a = F_thrust(t) - F_drag(v) - F_roll(v) - F_tether

F_drag = ½ρv²C_dA
F_roll = μ_bearing·N·(r_axle/r_wheel) + C_rr·N
F_tether = 2·μ_guide·T_tether

State vector: [x, v]
Time step: Δt = 0.0001 s
Stop when: x ≥ 20.0 m
